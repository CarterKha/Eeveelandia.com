Gym Leaders

The Top trainers of Galar!
The Galar region will revert back to the normal system of gyms, featured since the launch of the epic saga! In order to challenge the champion, you will have to earn a gym badge from all of the gym leaders throughout the region! Each gym leader specializes in a certain type of pokemon, and on defeat will reward a badge from their gym! Once you have gathered all 8 badges you will be able to challenge the pokemon league and champion, to become the new champion of the region!

Nessa is the Water Type Gym leader of the Galar Region. Her Main pokemon that is shown is an Drednaw that she can dynamaxe-
Drednaw is a Water/Rock type pokemon. It can have the abilities of Strong Jaw or Shell Armor. Visually it looks like a snapping turtle
Bea
Bea is the Fighting Type Gym leader of the Galar Region. None of her team is known yet, but personally i expect she will have a Machamp
Unlike in any previous games, Bea and Allister will have a "shared" gym, meaning that depending on which version you buy, you will fight a different leader
Allister
