The Sword and Shield Hype Squad

Scheduled to be released on Nov 11

Pokemon Sword and Sheild is the upcoming game in the Pokemon saga. In this game you will be exploring the region of Galar, thought to be themed on England. The starters to choose from are Sobble, Grookey, and Scorbunny. However, the new game comes with many new mechanics. Its been possibly confirmed that the tall-grass mechanics will be more similar to the mechanics of the Let's go pair. The star of new mechanics the new DynaMax, and GigantaMaxed Pokemon! Using this method you can temporarily power-up your pokemon from a few turns!
